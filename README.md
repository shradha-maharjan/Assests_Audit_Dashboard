I created a dashboard that tracks asset usage, highlights risk areas, and provides actionable insights for decision-making.

Step 1: Data Creation

I generated a synthetic dataset of thousands of healthcare IT assets, including both IT equipment and medical devices, with fields like status, department, usage dates, and warranty details.

Step 2: Data Preparation

I cleaned and transformed the data in Power BI using Power Query, creating calculated columns like idle days, warranty status, asset age, and lifecycle status.

Step 3: KPI Development

I created key metrics such as total assets, idle assets, critical idle assets, expired warranties, unassigned assets, and idle cost to quantify risk and inefficiencies.

Step 4: Dashboard Design

I designed the dashboard in a structured layout with filters, KPI cards, and visualizations to provide both high-level and detailed views of asset performance.

Step 5: Risk Analysis

I focused on identifying audit-relevant risks such as:

idle critical devices
expired warranties
assets beyond lifecycle
unassigned assets

Example:

Idle Assets → inefficiency
Critical Idle → operational risk
Idle Cost → financial impact

This dashboard helps organizations reduce costs, improve asset utilization, and ensure compliance by identifying underutilized assets and potential risks because:
- Idle assets represent wasted investment and potential inefficiencies in resource allocation.
- Critical assets directly impact patient care, so monitoring their utilization and condition is essential.
- It increases risk of failure and maintenance cost, especially for high-value or critical devices.
